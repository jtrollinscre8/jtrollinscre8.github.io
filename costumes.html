Costume Projects

To me, Halloween is just another opportunity to make something cool. This has allowed me to try new techniques and make a variety of costumes, some of which are shown below.

2020: Dragon

During the COVID-19 pandemic of 2020, I had plenty of time at home to design and make my own costume. I've always loved dragons, so I used plastic tablecloths, popsicle sticks, cardboard, and paper to make this ice dragon. Tape and hot glue were the primary adhesives.

2022: Darth Vader

I really grew to love Star Wars around 2022, and I found a Darth Vader helmet project online. Cardboard has always been my go-to material, so this was a perfect design, and I decided to make it into a Halloween costume. The lightsaber was a modified 3D print for the hilt, with a flashlight inserted inside. Then I used a tube light protector for the blade, covered with colored basket wrap.

Here is the helmet design I used.

2023: Stormtrooper

Continuing with the Star Wars theme, I decided to become a Stormtrooper for 2023 Halloween. The helmet was made in a similar way to the Darth Vader helmet, with some admitted creative liberties, and I created the rest of the armor from cardboard, basing it on pictures and this video. The gun was 3D printed from files from this design on Thingiverse. Overall, this was a very fun project to see come together.
